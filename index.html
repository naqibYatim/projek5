About Us

Who We Are

Malaysian contruction and hardware provider that specializes in developing IBS technology and infrastructure for modern residential. Driven by our passionate people, we persevere to create best service in bringing greater life style and value to our customers.

Kagumi draws its strengths from over two years of experience, working with customers from both the public and private sector.

  • Involved in fabrication

  • Supply high quality hardware
  • Best garment boutique
